SpeechAngel turns your phone into a hands-free device you control with your own
voice — commands you record yourself, in any language or speech pattern.

Unlike ordinary voice assistants, SpeechAngel does not try to understand words. You
teach it a handful of commands by example (a few recordings each), and it matches
your future speech against those recordings entirely on-device. That makes it:

• Language-independent — it works in any language, dialect, or mix.
• Inclusive of atypical speech — dysarthria, post-stroke change, fatigue, illness.
  Record extra examples under those conditions and it adapts to your own voice.
• Private — recognition is 100% on-device. No cloud, no account, nothing uploaded.
• Deterministic — each command maps to one fixed device action. No autonomous AI in
  the loop.

Teach → Try → hands-free: set up a few commands with a caregiver, try them, then turn
on always-on listening. A foreground service keeps a microphone-only listener running
so you can act without touching the screen.

Built for people who find touchscreens hard to use and mainstream voice assistants
hard to be understood by.
