# Autonomous Decision Log

Append-only record of high-confidence (>90/100) autonomous calls the agent made without asking —
the decision, the alternatives weighed, and the evidence. The trigger to add an entry: you made a
non-trivial design/process choice on your own judgment that a reviewer might later question.

Started **empty** (meta §5.1 — copy the format, not another project's content).

---

## Entry format

```
### <YYYY-MM-DD> — <short title>
- **Decision:** what was chosen.
- **Confidence:** NN/100.
- **Alternatives considered:** …
- **Evidence / rationale:** repo files or research findings (backtick code-paths).
- **Reversal trigger:** what would make us undo this.
```
